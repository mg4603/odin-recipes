Veg Club Sandwich

Description

Veg club sandwich

Ingredients

For Stuffing

  • 2 cups cabbage shredded
  • 1 cup capsicum sliced
  • 1 cup carrot shredded
  • 4 tbsp. mayonnaise
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper

For Sandwich

  • 3 pieces white bread
  • 1 + 1 tbsp. tomato ketchup
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 1 cucumber sliced
  • 1 tomato sliced
  • shredded cabbage as required
  • 1 tbsp. mayonnaise

Steps

For Stuffing

  1. Add cabbage, capsicum, carrot, mayonnaise, salt and pepper to a bowl.
  2. Mix everything well and keep it aside.

For sandwich

  1. Take 3 slices of white bread and cut the sides of the bread.
  2. Grill the bread slices.
  3. Spread 1 tbsp ketchup evenly on one slice of bread.
  4. Add prepared stuffing to the bread. Also add sliced cucumber and onions.
  5. Cover with another bread slice and add more stuffing on the bread.
  6. Add sliced tomatoes and cabbage on top.
  7. Take one more slice of bread and spread ketchup and mayonnaise on it.
  8. Cover sandwich with this bread slice and insert some toothpicks in the corner
  9. Cut the sandwich in half.
  10. Serve
