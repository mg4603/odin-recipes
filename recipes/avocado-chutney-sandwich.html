Avocado Chutney Sandwich

Avocado Cucumber Chutney Sandwich is an easy, healthy, and delicious sandwich recipe prepared with bread, avocados, cucumbers, and green mint chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp. cream cheese (mayonnaise or hung yougurt)
  • 2 tbsp. of green chutney
  • 1 large avocado peeled, pitted and sliced
  • 1 small persian cucumber cut into thin slices
  • 1 tsp. chat masala
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Apply green corriander mint chutney to a slice of bread and cream cheese to the other.
  2. Lay a layer of cucumber slices followed by avocado slices
  3. Cover, grill, cut in half and serve.

Tips & Variations

  • Place cucumbers on tissue before preparation to prevent sandwich becomming soggy.
  • Add a cheese slice while grilling.
  • Cream cheese can be replaced with mayonnaise or hung yougurt.
