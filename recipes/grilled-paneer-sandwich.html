Grilled Paneer Sandwich

Description

easy paneer sandwich recipe

Ingredients

For stuffing

  • 1 cup panner, grated
  • 2 tbsp. carrot, grated
  • 2 tbsp. capsicum, grated
  • 2 tbsp. corn, boiled
  • 1 tbsp. corriander, finely chopped
  • 1/4 tsp. red chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp. cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 2 tbsp. tomato sauce

For sandwich

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 tsp. green chutney
  • 1 tsp. mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp. butter

Steps

For stuffing

  1. Add paneer, carrot, capsicum, corn and corriander to a mixing bowl.
  2. Add chilli powder, cumin powder, salt and tomato sauce.
  3. Mix until combined.

For sandwich

  1. Spread green chutney over a slice of bread.
  2. Add 2 tbsp. of paneer stuffing.
  3. Cover with bread slice spread with mayonnaise.
  4. Grill, cut in half and serve.
