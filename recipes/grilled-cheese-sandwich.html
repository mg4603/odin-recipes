Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Classic cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread: Thicker sliced bread
  • Butter:1/2 table spoon per slice
  • Cheese:Slices + Mozerella

Steps

  1. Butter the toast spread 1/2 Tbsp butter on one side of each slice of bread
  2. Heat skillet - place a cast iron skillet over medium/low heat and immediately add 2 slices of bread with the buttered side down.
  3. Add cheese - stack the cheese on one of the slices of bread, cover it with the other piece of toast and flip the sandwich over. Cook for 6-7 minutes.
  4. Brown the toast - Continue sauteeing, until both sides are golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  5. Serve - Cut the sandwich in half on the diagonal to serve.
